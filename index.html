About me

I grew up in Te Waipounamu (South Island, NZ). I am proud to be of Ngāi Tahu decent as it gives me a deep connection to Aotearoa. In recent years I have been based over in Canada, where I had a realisation that I want to be in the tech industry. I didn't really want to go through 3 more years of University so I was stoked when I came across Dev Academy.

I also love the outdoors and have a profound love for #VanLife. This was my van just outside of Glacier National Park in the USA.
