Learning Plan

Gus Hawke & Dev Academy

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?
Wow, what a big question… My long-term career pathway is definitely entrepreneurship within the tech industry. In terms of a goal I guess it would be developing a valid idea and building a sustainable business model for the idea.


A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning
If I am curious about a particular topic I would spend time gaining a deep understanding effortlessly. I can get sidetracked easily and stuck down rabbit holes very easily so I’m often reminding myself to remain on track!


What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?
Asking the right questions. I am probably more confident than most when it comes to speaking in front of people, or socialising. However, asking the right questions or difficult questions is something I strive to improve on.


A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.
I will treat this course like a full-time job and take responsibility for my own outcomes. I So far in this course I have found Toggl and timeboxes very handy. This will keep me on track and hopefully improve my productivity. I have however found myself in the weeds and just going for a walk/run outside has instantly improved my headspace. So some sort of exercise or meditation will be key to my productivity and ultimately my contribution to the team. I do want to jump at opportunities to develop, though given the short time-frame we have here at Dev Academy I will prioritise learning over socializing… though both have its place!


A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.
I will firstly attempt to seek answers on google. Trial and error - write down the problem in plain english and then try and resolve this on my own. If not prevail I will scroll through Slack to see if the same question has been asked and if not pick up the courage to ask the class!


A description of what you expect from the facilitation team
I really like the Dev Academy’s flipped classroom learning style and ‘just in time’ learning strategy. So I expect guidance as opposed to silver spoon answers.
