Emotional Intelligence

The purpose of this post is to explain
  • 1. What is Emotional Intelligence?
  • 2. How is it different to IQ?
  • 3. Why is it important?



What is Emotional Intelligence?


Emotional Quotient (EQ) is the ability to manage your emotions effectively to reduce stress, improve communication, increase empathy towards peers, diffuse conflict and overcome lifeâ€™s obstacles more effectively.

How is it different to IQ?


Often or traditionally, we judge future performance based on Intelligence Quotient, based on analytical and reasoning skills - how well one can use information and logic to answer questions or make predictions. IQ is a score derived from several standardised tests, used to determine academic ability, useful for filtering out gifted minds or minds which require special learning needs.

Why is it important?


EQ is important for developing as a leader and effective team member. Being able to communicate effectively and empathise with people around you, leads to more productivity and collaboration with your team.
